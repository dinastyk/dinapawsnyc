About Dinapaws

Dinapaws is a premium pet sitting service in NYC. We believe in compassion, care, and connection—to pets and their humans. Our mission is to give pet owners peace of mind, knowing their furry friends are in good hands.

With years of experience and a genuine love for animals, Dinapaws is here to make your travels worry-free and your pets happy.
