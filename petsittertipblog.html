3 Tips on Choosing the Right Pet Sitter for Your Pets While You’re Away

1. Have a Meet and Greet

Even a quick 30-minute meeting in person can help ensure you’re choosing the right person for your pets while you’re away.

2. Pay Attention to Questions Asked (or Lack Thereof)

Experienced pet sitters will ask important questions about medications, triggers, routines, and other needs. It’s a good sign when they’re thorough.

3. Ask Them Questions!

I was once asked during a meet and greet, “How do you handle emergency situations with pets?” It was a fantastic question. Just the day before, a pet I was watching had stomach issues. I explained how I located nearby vets in advance, called the pet parents to confirm, and got the pet the care it needed without surprises on their vet bill.
